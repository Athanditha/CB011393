    • Dining in Hilton


    Among our seven dining options, you can find exactly what you're craving. Curry Leaf offers fresh seafood and outdoor dining; Graze serves international fare from open kitchens; and the L.A.B lounge is laid-back yet chic. Casual cuisine is served at the pool bar, light fare is served at the Stella Karaoke Bar, and room service is available.

    Cafe Kai

    Enjoy a wide range of Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner options tailor made to fit your taste buds or grab a drink whilst indulging in our unique bar bites menu.

    Emperor's Wok

    Discover a journey through the richness and flavorsome streets of Cantonese and Szechuan cuisine.

    Graze Kitchen

    Flavours of the world that travels directly to your palatte! Our all-day-dining restaurant, GRAZE Kitchen, is a destination of delicious choices from Chinese, Indian, Italian, Japanese, Mediterranean, Sri Lankan, Thai, and Turkish cuisines.

    L.A.B

    Designed with an "urban park" theme overlooking our lotus pond. Enjoy high tea, a quiet coffee, or cocktails while listening to live entertainment nightly.

    Stella Karaoke Bar

    Sing along to your favourite 60s, 70s, and 80s hits and enjoy the retro and rock music at this vibrant Colombo bar at the Hilton Colombo. Be spoilt for choice with the extensive drinks menu, sip an exotic mocktail, and choose a delicious light bite from the snack menu.

    sunsetblu

    EAT. DRINK and PLAY at Hilton Colombo’s all-day club, SUNSETBLU. Enjoy a dip in the pool or the afternoon with a refreshing cocktail or put on your dancing shoes and dance the night away with your favourite bands and DJ's!
