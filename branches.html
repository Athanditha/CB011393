    Hilton Colombo

    The Hilton Colombo is a 5-star hotel located in the heart of Colombo, Sri Lanka. It is a short walk from the city's most popular attractions, including the Pettah Bazaar, the Galle Face Green, and the National Museum. The hotel has 392 rooms and suites, all of which are spacious and luxuriously appointed. It also has a variety of dining options, including an all-day dining restaurant, a rooftop bar with stunning views of the city, and a spa.

    Location

    Hilton Yala Resort

    The Hilton Yala Resort is a luxurious hotel located on the southern edge of Yala National Park, overlooking the Indian Ocean. It offers 42 family-friendly villas, rooms, and suites, all with their own private pools. Guests can enjoy a variety of activities, including elephant safaris, spa treatments, and beachside dining.

    Location

    DoubleTree by Hilton

    Situated in Weerawila, 7.4 km from Tissa Wewa, Doubletree By Hilton Weerawila Rajawarna Resort features accommodation with free bikes, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre. Among the various facilities of this property are a garden, a terrace and a bar. The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi throughout the property.
